What inspired us....

SYNOPSIS
Jojo fulfilled a lifelong dream by moving from Korea to London in early 2023. While cooking a traditional Korean dish, ´Kimchijeon´, she will be connecting her thoughts about belonging and identity.
CHARACTERS
The main character for the episode ´The Taste of Kimchijeon` will be Jojo a 30- year-old Korean woman. In the future, this project could be turned into a series called ´The taste of´, where people from all nationalities speak about specific topics related to their identity and inner world of thoughts.
REALISATION
The best conversations always happen late at night in the kitchen! Combining a 4x3 aspect ratio with intimate close up shots of Jojos face and stylised yet ´home cooking´ style food close up shots, the short film will feel like an intimate conversation in the kitchen with a friend.

ABOUT THE DIRECTORS

Marlene Noack (she/her) DoP, Writer Past director and co-producer

Marlene, originally from Germany, will be the DoP and 1st AC on this shoot. In the past she has written and directed her own short films, worked as a sound recordist on small productions and worked as a camera trainee on a feature film.

Helen Charles (she/her) Director

After successfully producing and assisting several shorts and being DoP, London born Helen, will step into the role as director for this short as cooking content and the topic of food is close to her heart.

George Moylette (he/him)

Co- Producer, Sound, Post Production Focusing mainly on production and post-production, George has taken on multiple roles in those departments for smaller short film productions in the past. For this project, he has co- produced and will do the main part of the post-production process.

Majo Hernandez (she/her) Producer

2nd AC Having recently moved from Mexico to London, Majo is experienced in Production, Directing and Camerawork. For this film Majo will step into the role of Producer and 2nd AC.
